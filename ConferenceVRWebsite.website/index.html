This is our project

Conferences and webinars fail to accommodate numerous qualified candidates. Our project aims to help everyone, regardless of their social, biological or financial status, allow an opportunity to participate.
-------------
We reduce costs while enhance efficiency in terms of time, money and convenience by allowing everyone to attend.

Real time communication

Talk to other players in the room as if you are on the phone. Discuss different topics in real time regardless of your locations.

Real interactions

Walk around different conference rooms (different events/topic). You can see other players that are also in the same room as you.

Cost reduction

By attending a virtual reality conference, you can come in and out at your convenient. This will allow you to reduce travel cost.

Increased Efficiency

By eliminating the hassle of traveling, our product will end up saving time, and allow you to do be more efficient.
